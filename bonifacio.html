Bonifacio Day

Date: November 30

Overview

Born on November 30, 1863, Bonifacio is often referred to as the "Father of the Philippine Revolution" due to his pivotal role in initiating the fight for freedom. The day is marked by various ceremonies, parades, and educational activities that highlight Bonifacio's contributions to Philippine history and his enduring spirit of patriotism.
