New Year

January 1st

Overview

New Year's Day, celebrated on January 1st, marks the beginning of the new year in the Gregorian calendar. It is a time for fresh starts, resolutions, and celebrations worldwide. Traditions vary by culture but often include fireworks, parties, and special meals. Many people reflect on the past year and set goals for the year ahead. Common customs include making New Year's resolutions, which are promises to oneself to improve habits or achieve specific goals. In many cultures, it is also a time to spend with family and friends, sharing joy and hope for the future.
