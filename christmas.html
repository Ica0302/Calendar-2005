Christmas Day

Date: December 25

Overview

Christmas, celebrated on December 25th, is a major Christian holiday that commemorates the birth of Jesus Christ. It is a time of joy, giving, and togetherness for millions of people around the world. The holiday is marked by various traditions and customs, including decorating Christmas trees, exchanging gifts, attending church services, and sharing festive meals with family and friends. Christmas also has cultural significance beyond its religious roots, with many secular celebrations featuring Santa Claus, holiday music, and festive decorations. The season leading up to Christmas, known as Advent, is often observed with anticipation and preparation for the holiday. Overall, Christmas is a time to celebrate love, hope, and goodwill towards others.
