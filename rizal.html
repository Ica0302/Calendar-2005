Rizal Day

Date: December 30

Overview

Rizal Day, observed on December 30th, is a national holiday in the Philippines that commemorates the life and contributions of Dr. Jos√© Rizal, a Filipino nationalist, writer, and revolutionary. Rizal is considered a national hero for his role in inspiring the Philippine Revolution against Spanish colonial rule through his literary works, particularly his novels "Noli Me Tangere" and "El Filibusterismo." The day is marked by various ceremonies and activities, including wreath-laying ceremonies at Rizal's monument in Luneta Park, educational programs, and cultural events that highlight his legacy. Rizal Day serves as a reminder of the importance of patriotism, education, and civic responsibility in the pursuit of national freedom and progress.
