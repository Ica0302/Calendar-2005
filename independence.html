Independence Day/h1>

Date: June 12

Overview

Independence Day, celebrated on June 12th, marks the anniversary of a nation's declaration of independence from colonial rule or foreign domination. This significant day is often associated with patriotic displays, including parades, fireworks, and various cultural events that highlight national pride and heritage. Independence Day serves as a reminder of the struggles and sacrifices made by those who fought for freedom and self-determination. It is a time for citizens to reflect on their country's history, values, and achievements while fostering a sense of unity and national identity among its people.
